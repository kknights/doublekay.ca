About me

I'm a front-end web developer from Toronto Canada who specializes in creating responsive, standards-compliant, cross-browser friendly websites. I've solid knowledge of HTML5, CSS3, SASS, Photoshop, and Git, and a working familiarity with javascript and jQuery. I also have some experience with PHP, JSP, .less, node.js, require.js, Yeoman, AJAX, JSON, and SVN.

Over the better part of the past decade I've worked with a variety of advertising and client-side companies including Mindblossom (Toronto), Youthography (Toronto, freelance), Cossette Communications Group (Toronto), Shine Ltd (Auckland, freelance), M&C Saatchi (Auckland, freelance), SLI Systems (Christchurch), Bimm (Toronto), Baby Robot/kbs+ (Toronto), and TELUS (Toronto).

I'm an avid traveller and whenever I'm not in Toronto I'm out exploring the world with my trusty camera at my side. I have not-so recently returned from a 16-month stint in New Zealand, which has been followed up by adventures in Australia, Thailand, South Korea, British Colombia, Peru, France, the UK, Iceland, and Spain.
